Why Visit Tibet?

Tibet, known as the Roof of the World, is a land of stunning natural beauty,
rich culture, and spiritual significance. From majestic mountains to ancient monasteries, Tibet offers a unique and unforgettable experience for travelers.

Top Attractions

  • Potala Palace: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and former residence of
    the His Holiness the Dalai Lama.
  • Jokhang Temple: One of the holiest sites in Tibetan Buddhism, located in the heart of Lhasa.
  • Mount Everest: The world's highest peak, offering breathtaking views and trekking opportunities.
  • Namtso Lake: A pristine alpine lake surrounded by snow-capped mountains.
  • Ganden Monastery: An important Tibetan Buddhist monastery with stunning architecture.
